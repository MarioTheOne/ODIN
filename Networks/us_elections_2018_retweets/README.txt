The datasets are NetworkX DiGraph stored in gpickle format. The dataset is composed by tweets related to the US Presidential elections of 2016. The nodes represent users and an edge from A to B indicates A retweeted a tweet from B.

The dataset 'us_elections_2018_retweets' is for NetworkX 2.x and 'us_elections_2018_retweets_nx1' is for
NetworkX 1.x
